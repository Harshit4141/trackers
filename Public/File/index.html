Why our website?

-This project has been developed to manage the bus tracking system so that every student is on time without getting late.
-We got the motivation for our project by observing how much precious time the students studying in our college wasted by just waiting for the bus on their particular route, and also there is a lack of information for the buses which are travelling on their route for the students who have already missed their bus.
-It is very feasible Software for the faculties as well as for the students, they can access the bus location from anywhere. The only thing to do is to login into our application after the fee payment and then access will be available, the tracking software will be installed in the bus driver's phone and through that, the user can get the exact location of the bus.
-In this project, we are building an android application and a dynamic website that will help users in a better way. The application will be easy to use as we will provide time-to-time notifications and alerts as the user will log in to either our website or app.
